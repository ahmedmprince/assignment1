Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Emphasizing the development of digital infrastructure, innovation, and technology adoption across various sectors, including education, healthcare, government services, and business.

Industrialization

Accelerating economic development through diversification and modernization of industries such as manufacturing, agriculture, and services.

Sustainability

Prioritizing sustainable development practices, renewable energy, environmental conservation, and tackling climate change.

Human Capital Development

Improving education, skills training, healthcare, and social welfare systems to ensure a more inclusive and skilled workforce.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
